Strands Overview

Congratulations on completing your Senior High School journey! Now that you've finished your chosen strand, it's time to explore the wide range of courses available to you. Each strand opens up different academic and career pathways. Whether you're from the STEM, ABM, GAS, or HUMSS strand, this section will guide you in discovering college courses that align with your interests and future aspirations.

STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics)

The STEM strand is perfect for those interested in pursuing courses in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. Whether you're passionate about discovering the mysteries of the universe or innovating new technologies, the STEM strand will prepare you for courses like Engineering, Medicine, Computer Science, and more.

Explore STEM Courses

ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management)

For those who are interested in the world of business and finance, the ABM strand is the perfect foundation. Students in this strand can look forward to pursuing courses in Business Administration, Accountancy, Marketing, Entrepreneurship, and other related fields.

Explore ABM Courses

GAS (General Academic Strand)

The GAS strand is designed for students who want to explore a variety of subjects across different fields. If you're undecided or interested in multiple career paths, the GAS strand provides a flexible foundation for pursuing courses in Education, Communication, Social Sciences, and more.

Explore GAS Courses

HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences)

If you're passionate about understanding human behavior, society, and culture, the HUMSS strand is the right choice. It offers pathways to courses in Psychology, Sociology, Education, Law, and Journalism, among others.

Explore HUMSS Courses
