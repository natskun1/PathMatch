What is PathMatch?

A Course Guide for Senior High Graduates is an online platform designed to help senior high school graduates make informed decisions about their future college courses. Targeted at students in Cebu City, Philippines, the website provides a comprehensive and user-friendly guide that connects the students' completed senior high strands (STEM, ABM, GAS, HUMSS) with suitable college courses.

Learn More

Why PathMatch?

The goal of PathMatch is to help students make informed decisions about their future by providing them with a user-friendly platform. Whether you’re looking to explore different course options or search for universities that offer specific programs, PathMatch makes the process easier and more accessible for every senior high graduate.

By categorizing courses based on academic strands, students can easily navigate through relevant options and find the path that best suits their interests and career goals. PathMatch aims to be a comprehensive resource for navigating higher education in Cebu City.
